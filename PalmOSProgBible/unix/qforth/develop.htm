Development Environment:

Quartus Forth is developed using:

  • Pila 1.0 Beta 3 Fluff 6 (68000 assembler); source is pre-processed with M4
  • PilRC 2.0a (PalmPilot™ resource compiler)
  • Quartus Forth itself; library modules are written in high-level Forth!
