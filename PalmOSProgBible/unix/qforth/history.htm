History:

1997.7.18 Quartus Forth prototype development begins under the name 'PilotFORTH'.
1997.8.7 First beta test version of PilotFORTH released (0.1.0).
1997.8.26 PilotFORTH (0.4.0) successfully passes John Hayes' coretest.
1998.1.25 Last version of PilotFORTH (0.6.1) released.
1998.2.1 PilotFORTH development stopped.
1998.2.1 Quartus Forth development begins from the ground up.
1998.3.2 Quartus Forth (0.1.1) successfully passes John Hayes' coretest.
1998.6.10 First beta test version of Quartus Forth released (0.2.1B).
1998.12.28 Quartus Forth 1.0.0 released in both evaluation and registered versions.
1999.3.2 Quartus Forth 1.2.0 released.
