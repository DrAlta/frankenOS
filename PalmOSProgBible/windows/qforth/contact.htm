Support Contacts

Please address your support questions to Quartus Forth Support (support@quartus.net). Registered users, please provide your registration code and HotSync user name in your e-mail.

General questions and comments may be posted in the Quartus Forth Discussion Forum; this is also where new releases and updates are announced.
