LispMe Y2K compliance

Seriously, what do you expect?

But as it's fashionable nowadays to include a Y2K statement, here it is:

The Scheme programming language and thus LispMe has no idea of a date, so LispMe won't be affected by the transition from 1999 to 2000 (and not by the millenium one year later, either :-).

The only date-dependent LispMe function (date-time) relies on the Y2K compliance statement of 3COM itself and worked on all test cases.
